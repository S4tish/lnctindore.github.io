What is computer Engineering?

Computer Science Engineering is an integrated field of engineering that combines aspects of Electronic Engineering with computer Sciences.It is mainly bifurcated into two parts - Hardware Engineering and Software Engineering.As the name reflects , Hardware Engineering is all about constructing a small microchip for large scientific computer.Software Engineering covers designing software for various purposes. It is not easy to define Computer Engineering in a Statement due to the extensive nature of the field.Computer engineers undertake various responsibilities depending on the industries and their place of employment , ranging from the manufacturing of microprocessors to creating and designing computer program and algorithm Skills Required for software Engineer
